Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TransferX ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our file-sharing service.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the service:

  • Files: Files you upload are stored temporarily.
  • Metadata: We store file names, sizes, and upload times to generate download links.
  • Usage Data: We do not track personal user activity or IP addresses for tracking purposes.

3. File Retention

Files are automatically deleted from our servers 3 hours after upload. Once deleted, they cannot be recovered.

4. Security

We use standard encryption protocols (HTTPS) to protect your files during transfer. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
